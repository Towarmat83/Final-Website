Hello !
If you are here it is maybe because you want to know how to install Minecraft on your hardware ! But do not worry, you are at the good place !

This part explains how to install Minecraft on your computer, let's start !

Step 1: Going to Minecraft's website

  • Go to minecraft.net on your search bar
  • Click on the green button 'Get Minecraft' at the right top
  • Choose your plateform. For us, it will be 'Computer'
  • Now you can choose between two different plateforms: Java Edition or Windows 10 Edition
  • For the next steps, the guide will be on Java Edition

Step 2: Buying Minecraft for PC

  • The game costs actually 23.95€, if you already have a account click here or click on 'Buy Minecraft'. Then create your account so you will be able to use your account on any computer !
  • When your account is finished and you have bought the game, you will be able to download the game

Step 3: Downloading Minecraft for PC

  • Click on the green button 'Download' and the download will start. Wait the end of the download
  • When the download is finished, run the application and follow the steps

Step 4: Final result

  • This is the result you should have before the launch of the game

Congratulations !
You have successed to install Minecraft !
Now, let's play !
