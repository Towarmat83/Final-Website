Installing Minecraft

On this page

Hello !
If you are here it is maybe because you want to know how installing Minecraft on your hardware ! But do not worried, you are at the good place !

This part explains how installing Minecraft on your computer, let's start !

Step 1: Going to Minecraft's website

  • Go to minecraft.net on your search bar
  • Click on the green button 'Get Minecraft' at the right top
  • Choose your plateform. For us, it will be 'Computer'
  • Now you can choose between two differents plateforms: Java edition and Windows 10 edition
  • For the following, the guide will be on the Java edition

Step 2: Buying Minecraft for PC

  • The game cost actually 23.95€, if you already have a account click here or click on 'Buy Minecraft' then create your account so you will be able to use your account on any computer !
  • When your account will be finished and you will buy the game, you will be able to download the game

Step 3: Downloading Minecraft for PC

  • Click on the green button 'Download' and the download will start. Wait the end of the download
