This part will explain everything on the home menu of Minecraft.
The version of the game is 1.18.2.

Brief explanation:

    Minecraft's home menu is composed of 4 big parts:

  • Singleplayer

    Multiplayer

    Options

    Minecraft Realms
  • In the background, a random scenery is in a loop
  • There is the version of the game on the left-bottom, here v1.18.2
  • You can change the language of your game by this button
  • You can have access to the accessibility settings by this button

Singleplayer:

    You decide to start the game, to begin the aventure...

    Before being immersed in the game, you need to create your world.

  • You must give a name to your world.
    If you do not give a name, your world will be called 'New_World' by default.
    It can be rename later.
  • You must choose in which mode you want to play: Survival, Creative or Hardcore.
      Survival : Search for resources, craft, gain levels, health and hunger.
      Hardcore : Same as Survival Mode, locked at hardest difficulty, and one life only.
      Creative : Unlimited resources, free flying and destroy blocks instantly.
  • Fourth type of difficulty exists:
      Peaceful: Hostile mobs cannot spawn, you cannot lose hunger and you automatically restore your health.
      Easy: Hostile mobs sometimes spawn.
      Normal: Hostile mobs often spawn.
      Hard: Hostile mobs always spawn.
    Remark:
    Depending on the difficulty, differents parameters in-game can change, like the probability of villager to change into a zombie-villager after being killed by a zombie.
  • Allows Cheats means that you can use commands in-game. Every types of commands actually exists like /gamemode' which changes your gamemode or /experience which gives you experience.
    Remark:
    In Minecraft's community, using cheats are prohibited, do you really want to cheat in a game ? It's up to you !
  • This section was created by the 1.18 version. In fact, you can play with your textures in-game.
  • This section allows you to modify if you want a lot of parameters in-game like the cycle day/night or keeping your inventory after your death. By the command /gamerule, you can change during the game these parameters.
  • Each Minecraft world is unique and there are determinated by their seed. You can enter the number you want, the game will create your world with it. Some seeds are better than anothers so if you want to play in a specific seed, enter the seed in the form.
    With /seed, you can know the seed of your world and share it !
  • This section allows you to have a world with or without structures. There are many structures in Minecraft like villages or dunjeons.
    Playing without structures disable you to finish the game !
  • This parameter is very important because it allows you to choose between different types of world generations.
    Five type of generations exist:
      Default: Normal world with every type of biomes.
      Superflat: Flat world that you can customize with presets parameters. Very useful in creative.
      Large Biomes: Same as the Default but the biomes generated will be bigger.
      Amplified: World with a lot of mountains and floating islands. Requires a good computer.
      Single Biome: World with only one biome that you can choose.
  • Give you at the beginning a chest with some basic loot in it.
  • You can import settings from your device to play with your settings !
  • After setting your parameters, click on Create New World and your aventure starts !
    Click here to start the aventure !
  • If you click on Cancel, you will go back to the Home menu.

Multiplayer:

    You are actually in multiplayer's menu of Minecraft.
    Here, you can join servers and play online !

    Be careful to always check if the version of the server allows the version of your Minecraft.

  • By clicking on Add Server, you can create a slot reserved for a server.
    You can rename this slot and you should add the IP address of the server.
  • Same thing by clicking on Direct Connection but without the special slot.
  • After creating a slot for a server, you can edit it by clicking on Edit or delete it by clicking on Delete.
  • You can join a server by selecting it then clicking on Join Server or just by double clicking on it.
  • Refresh allows you to refresh the status and actualise each server on your list.

Options:

    You are actually in settings's menu of Minecraft.
    Here, you can modify whatever you want to be comfortable during the game !

    Depending on each version, this menu is subject to change.

  • The Field of View (FOV) is the open observable area a person can see through his or her eyes or via an optical device. In Minecraft, it corresponds to the view of your player. You can choose a scale between '30' and 'Quake Pro', '30' is means the smallest view and 'Quake Pro' the largest view.
    Tip: In 'Quake Pro', you are able to see wider than '30', it can be useful.
  • Skin Customisation allows you to customise your player by adding a hat, a cape, a jacket...
  • Video Settings allows you to adjust the quality of the graphics. In fact, you can adjust render distance, simulation distance, particules, shadows, clouds, brightness...
    This sections affects a lot your FPS (Frame Per Second) so it is very important to well adjust this section.
  • Language allows you to change the language of your game. This menu is the same as this button on the home page.
  • Resource Packs is a section where you can add resources packs from your device to the game. This option is available when you create a new world.
  • Online manages Realms notifications and servers listings.
  • You can manages all the music, sounds and device with Music & Sounds
  • Controls is one of the first settings you should change at the beginning of your game because you can modify and manage mouse and keys settings.
  • In Minecraft, you can talk with people who are in the same server as you. You can also enter commands. With Chat settings you can manage all the parameters in relation with the communication. For example, you can adjust the size and opacity of the chat.
  • Accessibility Settings allows you to modify some parameters who are not classified in others sections. This menu is the same as this button on the home page.

Minecraft Realms:

    Realms is a safe, simple way to enjoy an online Minecraft world with up to ten friends at a time.

    It supports loads of minigames and plenty of custom worlds! Only the owner of the realm need to pay.
